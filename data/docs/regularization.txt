Regularization is used to prevent overfitting by penalizing large weights.

L1 Regularization (Lasso):
Adds the absolute value of weights to the loss function.
Encourages sparsity in model parameters.

L2 Regularization (Ridge):
Adds the squared magnitude of weights to the loss function.
Prevents weights from growing too large.

Elastic Net combines L1 and L2 penalties.

Regularization improves generalization performance on unseen data.
